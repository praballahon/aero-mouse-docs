Aero Mouse - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Aero Mouse handles user information.

1. Overview

Aero Mouse is a Bluetooth remote control application that allows users to control compatible devices such as Windows PCs, macOS devices, TVs, tablets, and other supported devices using mouse, keyboard, and media control features.

The app is designed with privacy in mind.

2. Data Collection

Aero Mouse does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or sensitive user information.

The app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not track users
  • Does not collect location history
  • Does not upload any information to external servers
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not sell user data to third parties

3. Permissions Used

Bluetooth Permission

Bluetooth permissions are required to:

  • Discover nearby Bluetooth devices
  • Pair with supported devices
  • Establish and maintain Bluetooth connections
  • Send mouse, keyboard, and media control inputs

Location Permission

Location permission may be required by Android systems for Bluetooth device discovery and pairing functionality.

Aero Mouse does not access, collect, store, or track your physical location.

4. Third-Party Services

Aero Mouse does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

Aero Mouse does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

6. Security

Since Aero Mouse does not collect or store user data, there is no user data stored on remote servers.

Bluetooth communication occurs directly between your devices.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future to reflect app improvements or legal requirements.

Any updates will be published on this page.
